Why Allocation Matters

Diversification spreads risk across varied asset classes like stocks, bonds, and gold. While high-equity setups (like Buffett's 90/10) hunt long-term growth with heavy drops, balanced setups (like the Permanent Portfolio) sacrifice absolute peaks to shelter your money during economic crashes.
