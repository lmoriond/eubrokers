# EU Brokers

Transparent pricing for every budget.

## Overview
EU Brokers compares brokerage platforms and investing apps available in Europe, focusing on fees, deposits, withdrawals, regulation, and investor protection.

## Main page
- https://eubrokers.pages.dev/ - Homepage with the comparison table and summary statements.

## What this site covers
- Broker fee comparisons.
- Maintenance fees.
- Stocks fees.
- ETF fees.
- FX fees.
- Minimum deposits.
- Withdrawal fees.
- Inactivity fees.
- Securities transfer fees.
- Regulation and protection levels.
