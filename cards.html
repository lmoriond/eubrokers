Top Picks for European Residents

Review our curated list of the best digital and traditional banking cards. Compare features, foreign exchange markups, and monthly costs to optimize your personal finances.

Trading 212 Card

Best for Travel
Key Characteristics
  • Zero FX fees on transactions (watch out for Mastercard mark up)
  • 0.5% cashback (€23 max)
  • Free ATM withdrawals up to €400/month then 1%
  • 0.15% fee when converting funds manually
  • Virtual disposable cards available
Monthly Fee €0.00

Trade Republic Card

Best for Cashbacks
Key Characteristics
  • No additional FX surcharge on foreign-currency withdrawals; Visa’s exchange rate is used
  • Saveback is 1% on card spending, that it is tied to saving/investing plan (requires active saving plan)
  • ATM withdrawals are free above €100; below €100 there is a €1 fee
  • Virtual is free, Classic card typically costs €5 once.
Monthly Fee €0.00

Revolut Cards

Best for Security
Key Characteristics
  • Debit, Virtual and Credit cards available
  • Integrated directly with currencies accounts
  • ATM withdrawals in Standard Plan: €200 or 5 withdrawals then 2% or €1
  • Fx fees on Standard: 1% over 1000 € (each rolling month) + 1% fee on weekend (17hs Fri-18hs Sun, Eastern Time)
Monthly Fee €0.00
